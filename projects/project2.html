MBI

Modern Warm Apartment

2BHK Residence · Bangalore

This project explores a balance between modern minimalism and warmth. The design focuses on creating a clean spatial layout while introducing textures and materials that make the home feel lived-in and comfortable.

Design Approach

The layout was structured to maximise openness while maintaining functional zoning. Neutral tones, layered lighting, and controlled material use were key in achieving a calm and cohesive interior.

Living Area

The living space is designed to feel open yet warm. Soft finishes and controlled lighting create a balanced environment.

Bedroom

A calm and restful environment achieved through muted tones, integrated storage, and minimal visual clutter.

Scope

Full Interior Design & Execution

Materials

Laminate, Veneer, Quartz, Ambient Lighting

Duration

10 Weeks
