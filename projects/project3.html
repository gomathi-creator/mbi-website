MBI

Refined Urban Residence

3BHK Apartment · Chennai

A contemporary home shaped by clarity, proportion, and restraint. The design avoids excess, focusing instead on spatial balance, material consistency, and long-term usability.

Scope

Interior Design & Execution

Materials

Matte Laminates, Stone, Veneer, Warm Lighting

Duration

9 Weeks

Spatial Planning

The layout was refined to ensure smooth movement across spaces. Storage and functional elements were integrated to maintain clarity and reduce visual interruptions.

Material Language

A controlled palette of finishes was used to create continuity. Subtle contrasts in texture add depth without overwhelming the space.

Design Approach

The project focuses on achieving balance through proportion, light, and restrained detailing. Every element is designed to contribute to a cohesive and functional living environment.
